O₂Hb & HHb vs Power

By construction O₂Hb + HHb = tHb at every sample, so these traces are mirror images about tHb/2. The interesting signal is how they diverge as power rises.
Interpretation note — tHb dynamic range here is narrow (~11.6–11.9 g/dL across the full ride), which is typical for Moxy on a stable placement. The SmO₂ swing (~40–77%) carries essentially all of the physiologic signal; O₂Hb and HHb traces will track that swing with tHb modulating their absolute level.
